# Lipedema-Surgeon

A lipedema specialist is a healthcare professional with specialized knowledge in diagnosing and treating lipedema, a chronic condition characterized by the abnormal accumulation of fatty tissues in the lower body. The role of a lipedema specialist is crucial due to the complex nature of the condition, which is often misdiagnosed or mistaken for obesity or lymphedema.
